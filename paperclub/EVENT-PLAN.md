# Agentra Paper Club — Operational Plan
## Kolkata, India · Summer 2026

**Goal:** Produce a 30-person research dinner that feels indistinguishable in quality from the YC Paper Club in Mountain View. Off-the-record dinner, publicly posted talks, curated attendee list.

---

## 1. Event Format (YC-caliber)

| Element | Spec |
|---------|------|
| **Attendance** | 30 people max |
| **Papers** | 5 per night, 10 min presentation + 20 min discussion each |
| **Schedule** | 5:00 PM doors → 5:30 PM talks → 7:00 PM break → 7:15 PM talks → 8:30 PM dinner |
| **Talks** | Recorded, edited, posted publicly |
| **Dinner** | Off the record. No cameras, no tweets. |
| **Frequency** | Every 3 weeks (5 sessions = 15 weeks = ~4 months) |
| **Curator** | Ashwin Burnwal (Agentra Labs) |

### Paper Selection Process
1. Curator shortlists ~15 papers 10 days before each session
2. Attendees vote via private form (ranked choice)
3. Top 5 make the cut
4. Presenters volunteer/claim papers on RSVP
5. Curator assigns backup presenters if needed

## 5. Attendee Curation

30 seats. Breakdown:

| Category | Seats | How Selected |
|----------|-------|--------------|
| Presenters (that session) | 5 | Volunteer/assigned |
| Researchers (PhD/postdoc/faculty) | 8 | Application + prior publications |
| Builders (engineers/founders) | 10 | Application + shipping history |
| Students (exceptional) | 4 | Application + project/demo |
| Sponsor guests | 2 | Guaranteed by sponsor |
| Organizers + staff | 3 | Ashwin + 2 helpers |
| **Buffer** | 2 | Last-minute quality additions |

### Application Scoring Rubric
- **Curiosity signal** (40%): What are you building? What papers shaped your work?
- **Contribution potential** (30%): Would others learn from your presence?
- **Diversity** (20%): Mix of research vs. builder, geography, background
- **Energy** (10%): Do you seem like someone who argues constructively?

### Waitlist Strategy
- Maintain a ranked waitlist of 20
- Release spots 7 days before each session
- "You're in" email should feel personal, not automated

## 8. Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| **Low applications** | Seed first 10 attendees personally; fake scarcity if needed |
| **Presenter cancels** | Always have 1 backup presenter prepped; curator presents as last resort |
| **Venue falls through** | Always book backup venue 48hrs ahead |
| **Food quality poor** | Taste-test every menu 1 week before; keep backup caterer |
| **AV failure** | Test entire setup day before; bring backup phone + mic |
| **No sponsors** | Self-fund Season 1; ₹55K/session is manageable for Agentra |
| **Kolkata perception** | Lean into it. "Not in Bangalore" is the point. Kolkata has ISI, JU, heritage. |
