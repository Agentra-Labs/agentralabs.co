Content Pipeline

Every session produces 5 × 10-minute YouTube videos — professionally mic'd, framed, edited. These are not webinar recordings; they're dense research summaries with live Q&A, optimized for the "papers explained" audience that watches on 1.5× speed.

Conservative estimate: 1,000+ views per video in 30 days. At 25 videos across the season, that's 25,000 researcher-eyeball-minutes on your brand — before organic sharing, Hacker News, and citations in student notes.
