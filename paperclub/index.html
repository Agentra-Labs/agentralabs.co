Agentra Paper Club

Where researchers and builders meet.

30 spots 5 papers 10 min each Dinner included

Researchers and builders don't talk to each other enough. So this summer, we're starting the Agentra Paper Club to bring them together.

Every few weeks, we'll host a small group to present and discuss five new research papers. One person presents each paper for ten minutes and leads the discussion. Then we'll keep talking over dinner.

We'll post the talks online. The dinners stay off the record.

We're looking for builders who read papers and researchers interested in how things break in production. The first group will be under 30 people.
